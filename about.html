about me.


My name is Saurav Barua, and I am a passionate graphic designer who firmly believes that great design has the power to change the world. With 4 years of hands-on experience in the design industry, I’ve had the honor of collaborating with clients from diverse industries, creating designs that not only meet their needs but also resonate with their target audiences. My expertise lies in branding, packaging design, and social media poster design, where I bring ideas to life with a perfect blend of creativity, strategy, and attention to detail. What sets me apart is my ability to draw inspiration from the beauty of the world around me. I’m deeply passionate about horticulture, which gives me a unique perspective rooted in my background in landscape and aquascape design. The interplay of colors, textures, and forms in nature profoundly influences my work, enabling me to create designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional.

Photography is another creative outlet that fuels my imagination and enhances my design process. Through the lens, I capture fleeting moments, explore new perspectives, and experiment with light and composition—skills that seamlessly integrate into my design work. Whether it’s capturing the perfect shot for a campaign or using photographs to tell compelling stories, photography allows me to push creative boundaries. I believe in the power of storytelling through design, and my goal is always to create pieces that connect emotionally with people while serving a purpose.
